About SANCHAY

SANCHAY ($SNCY) is a decentralized savings revolution. Built on the Solana blockchain, it’s a secure, fast, and community-driven digital vault for long-term holders. With a fixed supply, locked liquidity, and transparent tokenomics — it rewards patience and purpose.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To build a global decentralized vault where savings are protected and grow independently.

Mission:
- Empower savers with decentralized tools
- Ensure transparency via public tokenomics
- Launch reward, governance, and NFT vault tokens
- Promote financial literacy through community campaigns
- Reward patience in Web3 world
